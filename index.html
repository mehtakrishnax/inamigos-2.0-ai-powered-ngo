Get to Know Us Better

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization registered with NITI Aayog (CSR-1), leading the digital transition of community care.

🎯

Our Mission

To create positive, long-lasting societal change through community empowerment, transparent volunteering channels, and intensive ground deployment.

👁

Our Vision

Uniting driven individuals to foster a more inclusive, compassionate, and empowered society, with a focus on serving underprivileged communities with dignity and care.

💎

Core Values

Fostering absolute programmatic transparency, operational integrity, unconditional community support, and rapid scaling in every operations center.

Bridging Gaps, Fostering Hope

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation has expanded from a local food relief taskforce into an accredited Section 8 non-profit organization. Licensed by the Central Government of India, we coordinate impactful corporate and social sector support.

With registrations including NITI Aayog (CSR-1), 80G, 12A, and standard ISO 9001:2015 certifications, our processes represent absolute financial integrity. Donors can comfortably claim legal tax deductions while directly tracking their funds' ground deployment.

Our Journey

Tracking milestones from community inception to 2026 technology deployment

2020

Inception & Pandemic Relief

InAmigos Foundation established on Sept 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla to mitigate community isolation during the global pandemic.

2022

Project Expansion

Formalized Section 8 non-profit status. Deployed Projects Seva, Bachpanshala, and Udaan across Chhattisgarh, feeding over 10,000 families.

2024

CSR Accreditation

Received NITI Aayog registration (CSR-1), 80G & 12A certifications. Scaled global training internships to support over 30,000 students globally.

2026

Smart Integration (2.0)

Pioneered InAmigos 2.0 with custom matching tools, automated resource grids, and responsive donor portal ledgers.
